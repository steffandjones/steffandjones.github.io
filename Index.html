About Me

Hi, my name is Steffan Jones, and I am a 400m athlete from the Brecon Beacons. I have been an athlete for as long as I can remember, my life essentially revolves around it. For the last four years, I had the pleasure of studying abroad in the States at the prestigous Harvard University. Here, I had access to world class facilities, including the best tracks, gyms and recovery personnel. We travelled weekly from state to state, racing against world class athletes. Before this, I raced internationally for Wales on multiple occasions, training under Darrell Maynard - and obtained a diploma in Nutrition and Sporting Excellence at Loughborough College.
